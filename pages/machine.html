CCM-2000 Series

Advanced Cashew Nut Cutting Machine

The CCM-2000 Series represents the pinnacle of cashew processing technology, designed and manufactured in Vietnam to deliver exceptional performance and precision in cashew nut cutting operations.

Our flagship model combines innovative engineering with traditional Vietnamese craftsmanship to create a machine that maximizes whole kernel recovery while minimizing operational costs.

Key Features

  • High-precision cutting blades for maximum whole kernel recovery
  • Automated size detection system for optimal cutting angle
  • Energy-efficient operation with low power consumption
  • Easy maintenance with quick-access panels
  • Compact footprint for efficient space utilization
  • Whisper-quiet operation below 70dB

Precision Cutting System

Our patented blade technology ensures clean cuts with minimal kernel damage, resulting in a higher percentage of whole kernels and greater profit margins for your operation.

Smart Detection Technology

Advanced optical sensors automatically detect nut size and orientation, adjusting the cutting angle in real-time for optimal results regardless of variations in your raw material.

Energy Efficiency

Designed with power conservation in mind, our machines use up to 30% less electricity than competing models while maintaining superior performance.

Easy Maintenance

Quick-access panels and modular components allow for easy cleaning and maintenance, minimizing downtime and extending the machine's operational lifespan.

Customizable Settings

Adjust processing parameters to accommodate different cashew varieties and desired output specifications through an intuitive control panel.

Safety Features

Comprehensive safety mechanisms protect operators and ensure smooth, worry-free operation in compliance with international standards.
